Kaolin (China Clay)

China Clay is a natural mineral found in partly decomposed granite, an igneous feldsparic rock. Chemically, kaolin is a hydrated aluminium silicate containing Al2O3.2SiO2.2H2O.It is a unique industrial mineral, which remains chemically inert over a relatively wide pH range and it offers excellent covering when used as a pigment or extender in coated films and filling applications.

Types of Kaolin (China Clay)

Levigated China Clay

Pure grade of China Clay products by the processes of levigation and magnetic purification. This offer pure grade of China Clay by removing every impurities like Silica, Iron, Titanium etc. to make our clay pure and accurate in composition.

Hydrous China Clay

Hydrous Kaolin is characterized by its fine particle size, high brightness and rheology. We develop fine and ultra fine grades of Hydrous Kaolin from our best reserves, for various industries including Paint, Paper, Printing Inks, Paper board, Rubber etc.

Calcined China Clay

Calcined Kaolin is a product developed after calcination of high purity of China Clay at medium to high temperatures depending on end applications for its customers. Calcined Kaolin is the most widely used product in various applications worldwide.
